POEM


APPLE

You can tell me what to say,
you can tell me where to go,
but I can't find a way to care
about what you have to show,

for we are only players
on a stage that's set aflame
and we've yet to find the people
who will stay on in the game.

And it's time for you to go,
before my heart loses steel;
Before I can find a reason
to just tell you how I feel.
