FOOTBALL

Football is the most exciting and popular game in the world. Football is almost 700-800 years old and has been the world's most popular game for over 100 years. The game was originally founded by the Greeks and brought to Britan by the Romans. Football is commonly known as Soccer in Australia and South Africa. The word 'Football' gets its name from the combination of two words. 'Foot' and 'Ball', where the players run and kick the ball with their feet. FIFA World Cup is a football tournament that is conducted once in every four years. FIFA stands for Federation Iternationale de Football Association.
